Arrest warrant issued against ward chair of KMC-4

Reporter

KATHMANDU, June 29: An arrest warrant has been issued against Dinesh Maharjan, ward chairperson of Kathmandu Metropolitan City (KMC)-4.

The district court of Kathmandu issued the warrant to arrest Maharjan, Ex-chair of KMC-4 Mohan Bahadur Bista and ex-ward member Dhurba Prasad Triphati and present them to the court.

A complaint was filed accusing these people of forging fake government documents while Bista was the ward chairperson.
